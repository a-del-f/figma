About me

My name is Alejandro and I am a digital artist. I am from Madrid, Spain. I make paintings with a dreamlike style, but also sometimes I want to try new things and also I like to try different methods at painting. My most used color is a golden colour. I want to help people to learn how to draw, because I know so much would like to draw but they don’t know how to represent their ideas
